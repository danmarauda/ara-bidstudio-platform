# S-TIER AI-NATIVE PLATFORM TRANSFORMATION PRD
## Product Requirements Document

### **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

This document outlines the transformation of the existing NodeBench AI platform into an S-Tier, AI-native productivity platform leveraging Google's Gemini 3 Pro and Gemini 2.5 Flash to their full potential. The transformation will establish a new standard for intelligent workspaces by combining atomic design principles with advanced AI orchestration, multimodal understanding, and predictive user experiences.

**Vision**: To create the world's most intelligent, adaptive, and intuitive AI-native workspace that anticipates user needs and seamlessly integrates AI into every aspect of knowledge work.

**Target Market**: Knowledge workers, researchers, product teams, and organizations seeking AI-powered productivity enhancement.

**Timeline**: 12-week transformation with phased delivery and continuous validation.

---

### **CURRENT STATE ANALYSIS**

#### **Existing Architecture Strengths**
- **Robust Foundation**: Convex backend with real-time capabilities and strong data modeling
- **Multi-Agent System**: Sophisticated agent orchestration with specialized AI agents
- **Document Intelligence**: Advanced document processing, analysis, and management
- **MCP Integration**: Model Context Protocol support for extensible tool ecosystem
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Streaming responses, live editing, and multi-user presence
- **Comprehensive Data Models**: 50+ tables supporting complex workflows and relationships

#### **Technical Debt & Limitations**
- **UI/UX Inconsistency**: Mixed design patterns without atomic design principles
- **AI Provider Fragmentation**: Multiple providers without unified optimization
- **Component Coupling**: Tightly coupled components limiting reusability
- **Performance Bottlenecks**: Suboptimal rendering and state management patterns
- **Limited Multimodality**: Underutilized AI capabilities for non-text content
- **Reactive AI Design**: AI assists rather than anticipates user needs
